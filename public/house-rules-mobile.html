Game Night
House Rules
An Official Supplement to the Constitution of the Third Space Treasury
Issued by the Treasury · v2 · Subject to Amendment by Popular Chaos
The Treasury recognizes that its citizens gather, from time to time, to engage in recreational competition of the tabletop variety. Whereupon such gatherings occur, coins shall not merely observe — they shall participate.

§ 1 — The Ante & The Mystery Pot

At the start of each Game Night, every attending citizen contributes one (1) coin to The Pot — a shared bowl of coins, shuffled and anonymous. No one knows whose coin is whose. No one knows what rarity or effect hides inside.

How The Pot Works

Pot coins are shuffled together so no one can identify individual coins. When a player earns a scan, they reach in blind, scan the coin, and discover what they pulled. The physical coin goes back in the Pot. Points are yours. The mystery stays alive.

§ 2 — Awards & Bonus Scans

Award winners earn a bonus scan from the Pot — pull a coin blind, scan it for points and a mystery reveal, then return it to the bowl.

🏆 Victor's Claim 1 bonus scan
Win a game round outright.
😂 Moment of the Night 1 bonus scan
Group votes for the funniest, most chaotic, or most memorable moment of the round.
🗡️ Best Betrayal 1 bonus scan
Social deduction games only. Group votes for the most devastating bluff, play, or double-cross.
📡 Bullseye 1 bonus scan
Wavelength only. Closest guess to the target.
😬 Consolation Prize 1 bonus scan
Last place in a full game. Nobody goes home without at least one scan. It is still embarrassing.
On Voting

Subjective awards are settled by quick group vote — majority rules, host breaks ties. Thirty (30) seconds max. The Treasury does not convene deliberation panels.

§ 3 — Scanning a Pot Coin

  1. Reach into the Pot and pull a coin at random — no peeking.
  2. Scan the NFC coin with your phone.
  3. Discover what you pulled — rarity, effect, the whole reveal.
  4. Select Earned as the claim type.
  5. Write a story. The chaos deserves documentation.
  6. Return the physical coin to the Pot.

All Game Night scans count toward season score, leaderboard rank, streaks, and badges.

§ 4 — End of Night: The Coin Return

At the end of the evening, every player who anted a coin goes home with a coin. No one leaves empty-handed.

The host decides the method:

Option A — Random Draw. Coins are shuffled. Each player draws one blind.

Option B — Winner's Pick. Players with the most bonus scans pick first. Remaining players draw in any order. Ties broken by host or rock-paper-scissors.

§ 5 — Story Requirements

Every Game Night scan must include a story. The app won't stop you from skipping it. Your fellow citizens will know.

§ 6 — General Provisions

6.1 Host Authority. The host holds final interpretive authority. All rulings are final until the next Game Night, at which point nobody will remember anyway.
6.2 No Coin, No Ante. Players without a coin may not participate in the Pot or earn bonus scans. They may still play games — they just can't earn Treasury points that night.
6.3 Game Compatibility. These rules layer on top of any tabletop game without modifying that game's rules. Awards distribute at the next natural break if timing conflicts.
6.4 Amendment. Rules may be amended by majority vote. Amendments take effect at the next Game Night.
